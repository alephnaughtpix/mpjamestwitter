My Bloody Valentine at Glasgow Barrowlands in 2008. It was the first rock gig I wrote earplugs. There were even free earplugs at the cloakroom!

Even then, I wasn’t prepared for my eyeballs to wobble in tune with the bass frequencies of the noise solo of “You Made Me Realise”.
